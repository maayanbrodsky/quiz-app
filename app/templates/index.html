Welcome to the American Yawp Quiz Generator. This app will allow you to quickly generate
quizzes according to specific sub-sections in the American Yawp online open-source textbook.
Go to the Questions page to get started. Just check the boxes for the questions you want and click the button.
The app will lead you to download page that will let you download a standard quiz for printing,
a Blackboard-formatted quiz for uploading, and an answer key. The app also reorders the answers
every time you generate a new quiz
